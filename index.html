ABOUT VAYAM

"VAYAM" is a sanskrit word that means 'We', here it represents the collective strength of the group of young, joyful and
enthusiastic artists. The group was formed in 2010, for the pursuing and alumni students of School of Fine Arts, IPS Academy,
Indore with an objective to give them and their art a new dimension and providing them a platform to showcase their talent in
the Art-world.We have been successfully exhibiting shows in various cities from all over the country for 10 years and as a result we
have received rewards and appreciation from the cities like Mumbai, Pune, Jaipur, Nagpur, Lucknow etc.

Now it’s time to take a step further, I am delighted to introduce ‘Vayam’ to the global platform of Internet and Social media.

When the whole world is facing a state of despair due to the epidemic, I think it’s the right time when Art can create a wave
of joy among the society.
I wish all the very best to all my students for the exhibition and I wish them a successful and
bright future.

Smt. Naveena Ganjoo
Founder of Vayam group
H.O.D. School of Fine Arts,
IPS Academy,Indore

Testimonials

It’s a visual treat to see the different works of art expressing many emotions through various forms, Colors
and styles. I am happy to see Art joining hands with the technology in the form of an online exhibition to
spread joy in this difficult situation of pandemic across the world. I hope we all will get hope and
strength by going through the works presented with utmost sincerity.
I appreciate all the efforts done by the group and wish them all the success in future.

Ar. Achal K Chaudhary
President
IPS Academy,Indore

minds healthy.

exhibition.
